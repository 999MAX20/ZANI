# CRM Mechanics Evaluation Grid

## Acquisition and qualification

- Capture preserves source, Business, owner/responsible user, consent/context, and duplicate handling.
- Qualification produces a clear status, reason, next action, and conversion path.

## Pipeline and revenue

- Deals have business-owned pipelines/stages, controlled won/lost/reopen transitions, loss reason, amount history, and stale-next-action visibility.
- Forecasting derives from real open/won/lost data and explains its scope.

## Daily execution

- Tasks, appointments, inbox, notifications, and dashboard expose prioritized work with responsible users and deadlines.
- Activity timeline and audit make important changes traceable.

## Management

- Managers see permission-scoped workload, funnel, response, conversion, loss, overdue, and connector health metrics.
- Reports answer operational questions instead of presenting vanity totals.

## Automation and AI

- Automations are business-scoped, idempotent, observable, and use domain services.
- AI cites permitted sources, reports missing data, stays optional, and requires confirmation/approval for critical mutations.

## Completeness gate

A capability is not complete when only a model or endpoint exists. Require a reachable workflow, permissions, failure/no-data state, audit or events where needed, and proportional tests.
